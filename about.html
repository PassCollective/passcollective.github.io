PASS Collective is a startup applying football as a tool for development on and off the field. On field, we actively work on football excellence and life skills development as a team. Off field, we apply our unity as a football team to community user-centred design projects and academics.

The long term goal of PASS is for our children and youth to meaningfully seek livelihood opportunities in the sport and creative sectors in which they are empathetic, skilled and find tremendous joy. Overall, PASS Collective is a network of people and organisations that believes in the importance of creative intelligence.
